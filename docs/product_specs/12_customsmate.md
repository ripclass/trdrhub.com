## Problem Statement
Customs declarations are painful:
- Complex forms (UK: 54 fields, EU: 76 fields)
- Different systems per country
- Frequent changes (Brexit, ICS2)
- Errors cause delays and penalties
- Customs brokers charge $50-150 per declaration

## Solution
An intelligent customs declaration assistant:
- Guided form completion
- Auto-populate from commercial docs
- Real-time validation
- Direct submission to customs systems
- Status tracking
