## Overview
**Product Name:** TRDR Trade Analytics  
**Tagline:** "Insights that drive smarter trade decisions"  
**Priority:** MEDIUM (Enterprise upsell, data moat)  
**Estimated Dev Time:** 5-6 weeks  

---

## Problem Statement
Traders lack visibility into their operations:
- No consolidated view of trade performance
- Can't identify patterns in discrepancies
- Don't know which routes/partners perform best
- Manual reporting takes hours
- No benchmarking against industry

## Solution
A comprehensive analytics dashboard showing:
- Trade volume and trends
- Discrepancy patterns and root causes
- Partner performance scoring
- Processing time analysis
- Cost optimization insights
