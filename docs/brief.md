# LCopilot Beta Brief

This file is a short top-level brief for the LCopilot Public Beta.

Detailed product truth lives in `docs/brief/`.

## Current brief summary

- LCopilot is shipping as one SME trade-finance product spine.
- Exporter is the gold path.
- Importer is in scope, but it must reuse the same auth, validation, and result infrastructure.
- Bank is parked from launch-critical scope.
- The validation core is the strongest asset in the repo.
- Auth, onboarding, and routing trust are the biggest blockers.
- The beta is public, English only, and uses a hard paywall with an initial free-entry allowance.
