Critical (2)

Invoice total does not equal the sum of line items
Rule ARITHMETIC-1 · Doc: commercial_invoice
LC clause

45A: TOTAL VALUE USD 61,700.00 CFR REDACTED PORT

Expected

Sum of quantity x unit price across all line items = stated invoice total

Found

Line items total USD 60,950.00; invoice states USD 61,700.00 (difference USD 750.00)

Suggested fix

Reissue the commercial invoice with the total corrected to the line-item sum, or correct the unit price on item 3 if the stated total is the intended contract value.

Bill of lading not marked CLEAN ON BOARD
Rule UCP600-27 · Doc: bill_of_lading
LC clause

46A: FULL SET OF CLEAN ON BOARD OCEAN BILLS OF LADING

Expected

An on-board notation with no clause declaring a defective condition of goods or packaging

Found

B/L carries 'SHIPPED ON BOARD 12 JUN 2026' but no CLEAN notation; margin remark 'two cartons re-taped' present

Suggested fix

Request the carrier reissue the B/L without the defective-condition remark, or obtain the applicant's written waiver before presentation.

Major (2)
